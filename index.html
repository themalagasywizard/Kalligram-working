Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI writing assistant work?

Our AI assistant uses advanced language models to help you generate ideas, overcome writer's block, and improve your writing. It learns from your style and story context to provide relevant suggestions.

Can I export my stories?

Yes! You can export your stories in multiple formats including PDF, EPUB, and Word documents. All your work is also automatically saved in the cloud.

Is my work secure and private?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data, and your work remains 100% private and owned by you.
